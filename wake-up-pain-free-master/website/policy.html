Privacy Policy Summary

Commitment
KlassChiropractic.com and Klass Chiropractic P.C. (collectively, “Company”) is committed to the privacy and dignity of our community. We take your privacy seriously and are dedicated to handling any personal information you provide with the utmost care and respect.

Privacy
Company will never share, disseminate, rent or sell your personally identifiable information – including name, postal address, e-mail address and telephone number – with or to third parties, including advertisers or sponsors, except when we have your permission or under the circumstances set forth in detail in our full Privacy Policy. Company will use this information to provide you with our services and/or products and to offer you promotional materials from or through Company, as well as for operational and administrative purposes.

Advertisements
Company may advertise services and/or products online on behalf of third parties on the Company website and provide you with the opportunity to “opt-in” to and “opt-out” from e-mails from Company or specifically identified third parties. No personally identifiable information will be shared with these advertisers, sponsors or any other third party that is not an affiliate of Company.

Data Aggregation
Company may combine demographic and other information about our users, obtained from registration forms and/or user profiles, to create “aggregate data.” All personally identifiable information will be removed from this data before disclosing such data to third parties. Aggregate data disclosed to third parties will not contain any information that can be used to identify or contact our users.

Website Usage Tracking
Please note that Company may contract with a third party to help manage and optimize our Internet business and communications. Website usage tracking services help websites measure how visitors use and navigate a site and the effectiveness of advertising by using web beacons and/or cookies. The type of data that may be collected includes, but is not limited to, the pages visited and links clicked by users. Although such a third-party company would log the information coming from our site on our behalf, we would control how that data may and may not be used.

Protection of Protected Health Information (“PHI”)
Company is committed to protecting your PHI. PHI is information about you that may be used to identify you (such as your name, social security number or address), and that relates to (a) your past, present or future physical or mental health or condition, (b) the provision of health care to you, or (c) your past, present, or future payment for the provision of health care. In conducting its business, Company will receive and create records containing your PHI. Company is required by law to maintain the privacy of your PHI and to provide you with notice of its legal duties and privacy practices with respect to your PHI. Note that Company’s full Privacy Policy explains your rights concerning PHI and circumstances where Company may disclose your PHI or seek your permission to disclose your PHI.

Manipulation Under Anesthesia ("MUA") is a multi-specialty approach to treating chronic musculo-skeletal pain. A licensed physician and a licensed chiropractor are both involved in the overall procedure – the physician is solely responsible for determining the medical appropriateness and ordering, as well as administering of anesthesia while the chiropractor is solely responsible for performing the necessary manipulations while the patient is "asleep." Patients should be aware that there is no direct or indirect financial relationship between the physician and chiropractor who are involved in the MUA procedure. The physician is a Board-certified anesthesiologist and the chiropractor is a licensed practitioner who has received specialized training in how to perform MUA.
