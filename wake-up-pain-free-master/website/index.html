What We Do.

Klass Chiropractic, P.C. treats pain caused from all spine related issues with Manipulation Under Anesthesia, including back, neck, shoulder and arm/leg pain.

What is Manipulation Under Anesthesia?

MUA is a conservative multi-specialty approach to treating chronic spinal pain using minor doses of anesthesia. The use of light sedation allows for the practitioner to painlessly impact and remodel restrictive scar tissue that would otherwise be too painful to treat.

Manipulation Under Anesthesia ("MUA") is a multi-specialty approach to treating chronic musculo-skeletal pain. A licensed physician and a licensed chiropractor are both involved in the overall procedure – the physician is solely responsible for determining the medical appropriateness and ordering, as well as administering of anesthesia while the chiropractor is solely responsible for performing the necessary manipulations while the patient is "asleep." Patients should be aware that there is no direct or indirect financial relationship between the physician and chiropractor who are involved in the MUA procedure. The physician is a Board-certified anesthesiologist and the chiropractor is a licensed practitioner who has received specialized training in how to perform MUA. Klass Chiropractic, P.C. does not guarantee or warrant that patients will actually ‘wake up pain free.’ This is simply a marketing phrase to convey to potential patients who are appropriately screened and treated through MUA that they are reasonably likely to see a reduction in their pain over time.
