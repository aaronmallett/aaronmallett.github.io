Campground

Located right off of the I-29 exit 127 in Sloan, Iowa. The Place RV Park is nearby Browns Lake, Snyder Bend, and Blue Lake offering fishing and boating opportunities.

The Place RV Park offers many amenities including:

  • Full bath and showers (truckers welcome)
  • Landromat with stools
  • 30 and 50 amp available at 110v
  • Tent spots
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • City of Sloan water and sewer
  • Monthly and nightly rates
  • City of Sloan Fire and Rescue.
